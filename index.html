5W1H Framework

Every strategic initiative is analyzed through six critical dimensions:

WHO

Stakeholders, decision-makers, owners (RACI), affected groups, partners

WHAT

Problem scope, success metrics, initiatives, target outcomes, deliverables

WHEN

Timeline, milestones, dependencies, critical path, phasing

WHERE

Business units, regions, segments, systems, processes impacted

WHY

Root causes, strategic rationale, quantified benefits, value drivers

HOW

Workplan, enablers, capabilities, resources, change management
